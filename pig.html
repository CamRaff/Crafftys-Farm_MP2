Piper the Pig


Congratulations on finding Piper the Pig!


For all of your hard work, here are 3 fun facts about Piper and their other pig friends!

  • Fact 1 - Pigs are very clever! Pigs are actually more clever than dogs, and can learn their name, learn to sit and jump, and even play fetch!
  • Fact 2 - Pigs are actually very clean! You may think because they're often covered in mud they're smelly, dirty animals, but they actually do this to stay cool and protected from the sun!
  • Fact 3 - Pigs are LOUD! An adult pig can make a noise reaching up to 115 decibels, which is nearly as loud as a clap of thunder! OINK!

Charlie the Chicken


Congratulations on finding Charlie the Chicken!


For all of your hard work, here are 3 fun facts about Charlie and their other chicken friends!

  • Fact 1 - Chickens are dinosaurs! Just like you share DNA with your parents, a chicken shares their DNA with a T-Rex! That makes them a living relative of dinosaurs!
  • Fact 2 - Chickens can see better than humans! Chickens can see colours, just we can. However, they can also see something that humans can't, and that is ultraviolet! Ultraviolet is something humans can only see when they use a special light.
  • Fact 3 - Chickens love a bath! Just like us, chickens like to keep themselves clean. There is one big difference though, and that is chickens actually bathe in dirt and dust! Stinky!

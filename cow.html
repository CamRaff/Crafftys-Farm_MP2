Coco the Cow


Congratulations on finding Coco the Cow!


For all of your hard work, here are 3 fun facts about Coco and their other cow friends!

  • Fact 1 - Cows are friendly! Just like you and me, they are sociable creatures. They often huddle together in a herd, meaning they are always with their friends!
  • Fact 2 - Cows can be picky! They may spend time with their friends, but they can also choose their friends. Sometimes they stay away from other cows they dont like!
  • Fact 3 - Cows play games! Just like us, cows like to have fun. They often find themselves running around and also even playing hide-and-seek! Peek-a-boo!
